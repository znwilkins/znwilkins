Canada Post Delivery Truck Wrap

June 2016.
Vehicle Wrap. Screen and digital print, with computer cut vinyl.

This project involved wrapping 235 delivery trucks across Canada.

I was responsible for setting up the artwork for proof, and print,
and then supervising the project as it was produced.

Hudson's Bay Motorhome

June 2013.
Vehicle Wrap. Digital print, with computer cut vinyl.

This project, while featuring a simple design, was deceptively challenging.

The entire project was completed in one week, from proof to installation.
Colour match was paramount because I was using two wide-format digital printers.

Hockey Night: Play On! Trailer

May 2013.
Vehicle Wrap. Digital print, with computer cut vinyl.

This project is a personal favourite, because it's just plain cool.

The design on this wrap featured many shadows and transparencies,
which often cause trouble for raster image processors.
To combat this, I printed many of the components separately,
which were then trimmed to size.
