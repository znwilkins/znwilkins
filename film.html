When I'm directing I prefer it to be a documentary,
while art direction was my passion in fiction film.

After two years at Ryerson University's School of Image Arts,
I realized that filmmaking needed to be a personal passion.

Rusty

April 2015.
Short Documentary. Role: Director.

This film came about as a result of a character profile.

It explores the life and outlook of (now) Bishop Eric Reynolds, of the Old Roman Catholic Church,
and how his rough and tumble upbringing informs his sermons.

Flight

October 2015.
Short Narrative. Role: Co-Director.

This film was intended to evoke the style of Nicolas Winding Refn's Drive.

It tells the story of a young man, who must go on the run from shady individuals,
and his girlfriend, who decides to make flight with him.
